## Overview
This Power BI dashboard provides comprehensive insights into food delivery sales and customer behavior for Omato Food Delivery. The interactive visualizations help monitor order trends, product performance, payment methods, and delivery status across different user segments and time periods.

## Key Features
- **Total Sales Metrics:** Displays total quantity sold, number of transactions, and average quantity per order.
- **Monthly Sales Trend:** Bar chart shows total quantity sold by month to track seasonality.
- **User Segmentation:** Transactions broken down by food type and member type (Gold vs Regular).
- **Product Popularity:** Horizontal bar chart highlights the top-selling food items.
- **Delivery Status:** Pie chart visualizes the proportion of delivered vs canceled orders.
- **Payment Methods:** Breakdown of transactions by payment mode including UPI, COD, and card.
- **Time Series Analysis:** Line chart tracks transactions by year and month for trend analysis.
- **Dynamic Filters:** Slicers for month, member type, payment mode, and food type allow granular data exploration.

## Data Description
The dashboard utilizes order data containing:
- Order month and date
- Member type (Gold, Regular)
- Payment method (UPI, COD, Card)
- Food type and individual items
- Delivery status (Delivered, Canceled)
- Quantity ordered and transaction counts

## Purpose
- Identify sales trends and peak months for better inventory planning.
- Understand customer preferences by food type and membership.
- Monitor delivery performance to reduce cancellations.
- Analyze payment method distribution to optimize checkout experience.
- Provide actionable insights to marketing and operations teams.

## How to Use
1. Open the `.pbix` file in Power BI Desktop.
2. Use the slicers on the left panel to filter data by month, user type, payment mode, and food category.
3. Hover over charts to view detailed tooltips.
4. Share insights with teams to support decision-making and strategy.
